Fu da PIE

OVERVIEW : Fudapie —- the fluffy manager of this home. She's a cat with clear priorities: naps first, everything else later. She considers the keyboard her personal throne, your lunch her appetizer, and every closed door a personal insult. Fudapie may not understand gravity, but she fully understands the sound of a treat bag opening. She runs this house—we just live here. Professional biscuit maker, part-time cloud watcher.

Whose little paws?

Tiny, pink-beaned clouds with hidden destructive power. They knead blankets like a professional baker, yet somehow always land squarely in my morning coffee.

Night sleeper

Curled into a perfect cinnamon bun, one paw draped over her eyes, dreaming of world domination—or maybe just that tuna she saw earlier. Silence follows her like a shadow.
